About Openza

Openza is an open source initiative focused on creating modern, beautiful, and secure productivity tools. Our mission is to simplify task management by bringing together multiple task providers into unified, native desktop experiences.

Built with modern web technologies and distributed under the MIT license, all our projects are community-driven and transparent in development. We believe in security by design, with OAuth 2.0 authentication, encrypted storage, and strict privacy standards.
